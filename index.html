Interesting Facts About Camels!

  1. Camel's Ears Are Furry
  2. Camels have thick eyelashes to protect their eyes from the Desert dust
  3. Camels can endure up to two full weeks without drinking water.

Interesting Facts About Chameleons!

  1. Chameleons have eyes that can swivel.
  2. Sick chameleons look pale in color.
  3. Chameleons mostly live in Africa, Asia, and southern Europe.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon!

  1. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  2. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
  3. Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph.

Interesting Facts About Teddy Bears!

  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  3. The world's smallest stitched teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall.

Interesting Facts About Pandas!

  1. Pandas eyes are different to normal bears.
  2. Pandas spend a lot of time in their day eating.
  3. Bamboo is critical to a panda's diet.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas!

  1. They make a sound like a toy frog.
  2. Some cicadas are quite small.
  3. Cicadas are underground builders.
